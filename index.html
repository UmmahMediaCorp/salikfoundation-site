Our Mission

To illuminate lives with the Qur'an's guidance.

Guided by compassion, justice, and service to humanity, the Salik Foundation spreads the message of the Qur'an and provides care, education, and opportunity for orphans around the world. Two missions, one purpose — to lift hearts, and to lift lives.
