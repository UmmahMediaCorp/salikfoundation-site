The opportunity

Few are chosen to carry the Qur'an to those who have never met it. Today, you can be one of them.

When you place a Qur'an in the hands of someone discovering Islam, every verse they read, every prayer it inspires, every heart it softens — flows back to you, by the mercy of Allah. The reward does not end when you give. It begins.

The Prophet ﷺ said: "Whoever guides someone to goodness will have a reward like the one who did it." There may be no greater good to guide a soul toward than the Book of their Lord.
