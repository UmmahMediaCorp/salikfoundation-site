Our mission

To place the Qur'an in every willing hand — and carry its light to every corner of the earth.

For fourteen centuries the Qur'an has guided hearts out of darkness. Yet many have still never held a copy of their own. Salik Foundation exists to change that — printing beautiful mushafs and giving them, freely and faithfully, wherever they are needed most.

The journey

From our hands to theirs.

Every Qur'an we give travels the same path — from a quiet press, to a community far away, to a single pair of waiting hands.

01

First

We come to the community

Salik travels to towns across Canada, the United States and beyond — setting up our Qur'an stands where people already gather, and opening the door to the Book of Allah.

02

Then

We share the message

In masājid and community halls, we speak to the heart — the mercy, the guidance and the beauty of the Word of Allah — and invite every soul to discover it for themselves.

03

Finally

We place it in waiting hands

Free copies of the Qur'an — for elders, families, and children holding the Book of their Lord for the very first time. The moment they open it, your reward begins.
